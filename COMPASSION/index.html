Abundant Life Child Development Center

Abundant Life Child Development Center is a project of Compassion International. It has a vission of shaping life tommorow's leaders.
As part of an international Christian missionary NGO, Abundant Life Child Development Centre is a holistic child development program dedicated to breaking the cycle of poverty for vulnerable children. Our core mandate is to empower children cognitively, socially, emotionally, and spiritually, equipping them for a brighter future.

Through comprehensive support, we provide:

Education assistance and resources

Youth empowerment programs

Spiritual guidance and mentorship

Socio-emotional development opportunities

Our goal is to help children reach their full potential, becoming confident, compassionate, and contributing members of their communities. By addressing the root causes of poverty and inequality, we strive to create a brighter, more abundant life for generations to come
